Pronanosomes and nanoparticles


PRONANOSOME NIO-N

Pronanosome Nio-N formulation is designed to obtain non-ionic surfactant vesicles (niosomes) which are known for their stability and versatility and can be employed in multiple applications.

Learn more
PRONANOSOME PEGylated (Stealth)

PEGylated nanovesicles have high stability due the sterical repulsion. The presence of PEG on the surface of the nanovesicles has been shown to extend blood-circulation time while reducing mononuclear phagocyte system uptake (stealth nanovesicles).

Learn more

Pronanosome-GF

Fluorescent nanovesicles are very useful to in vivo/in vitro/in situ/ex vivo nanovesicle detection.
Pronanosome green fluorescence series allow to obtain fluorescently labelled nanovesicles (Green fluorescence) in a fast and easy way.

Learn more

PRONANOSOME LIPO-N

Pronanosome Lipo-N formulation is designed to obtain liposomes from natural phospholipids.
Liposomes are the most common nanovesicles and can be used in several applications.

Bioquochem S.L. is a biotechnology company founded in 2015 which main purpose is the development of biological kits and the design of portable devices for measuring biological parameters.

Bioquochem's main goal, is to design, develop and produce simple and reproducible methods for researchers to carry out their research easily. Bioquochem's specialized team has experience in oxidative stress and its relationship to various diseases such as cancer or cerebrovascular diseases. Furthermore, Bioquochem S.L. offers a wide range of services for measuring biological parameters such as protein carbonylation, thiol status, antioxidant activity, etc.
