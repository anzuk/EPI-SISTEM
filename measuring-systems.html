MEASURING SYSTEMS

Measuring can be a complex activity related to a physical, chemical, electrical or electronic value. Measuring systems need to identify the value, measure it, or characterize it by comparison, and give apropiate, and useful, information to the user.

Keeping in mind that we have both electric and also non-electric values, the translation/evaluation/comparison is made by a sensor and we often find ourselves reading a digital number and a measuring unit. EPI SISTEM can provide all the components of a measuring chain: sensors, conditioning circuitry, AD cards, PC or controllers, software and also DA cards. We are also specialized in customized control and automation systems related to research, education and industry.

WHY CHOOSE NI DAQ?

You depend on measurements to make key decisions and discoveries. Avoid missing critical data with limited tools that can’t meet your specific application needs. Get to the right decision faster with a customizable and accurate measurement solution and global support from NI.
